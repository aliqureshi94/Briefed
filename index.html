Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't this more expensive?

We've created processes which help us automate many tasks, thereby enabling us to lower costs.

Do you provide high-resolution files?

You'll receive all high-resolution files and complete rights to use these wherever you want.

Is there any minimum contract term?

For graphic design services, nope. For websites, we need a minimum 6 month commitment.

How does the process work?

We'll send you a brief to complete (don't worry, it's not lengthy) to understand your requirements, submit this back and we're ready to go.

How long does it take to produce designs?

This depends on the complexity of the task, usually landing pages and UI will take between 1-3 days, the same goes for graphic design work.

I have several tasks, how many can you handle?

To sustain our business model, we accept one task at a time, once completed, we'll start the next one.

I don't like the design - can I get a refund?

Yes - we have a 10-day 100% money back guarantee.

Can you code a website for me?

Yes we can, we can work with pure HTML or WordPress sites, we not do e-commerce, yet (we're working on this in the future.)
